About Work

Yash Shukla

I'm a portraiture photographer, I take pictures of people.

Born in Lucknow, studying in Dehradun. My first years were spent behind the iron curtain. I now live in Dehradun. I like travelling.

My goal is to show the beauty, grace, strength and enthusiasm of people. I focus on non-models and place regular people in exaggerated situations that highlight the aspect of who they are. I look for a sense of sefiance and fortitude in my work.

I'm based in Dehradun but travel around India for projects around India. I shoot a lot of portraiture as well as assignments for agencies.
